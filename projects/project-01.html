Conceptual – Body Device – “Stilts”

2021

Set in a world where the ground itself becomes unstable terrain, this project imagines a survival apparatus designed for movement across shifting, contaminated, or uninhabitable landscapes. The Body Device functions as an extension of the human body—an exoskeletal stilt system that lifts inhabitants above toxic surfaces, unstable soils, and atmospheric hazards.

Developed through iterative models and patent-style drawings, the device operates as a narrative tool as much as a physical prototype, speculating on how future bodies might adapt, augment, or re-engineer themselves to navigate hostile ecologies. It proposes mobility not as a given, but as a constructed condition of survival.
