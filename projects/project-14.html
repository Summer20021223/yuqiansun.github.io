Interactive Space – “In-Formation”

2025

This project transforms a linear corridor into an interactive space of movement, encounter, and gradual evolution. The ramp reimagines circulation as a social landscape, where ascending and descending shift perspectives, modulate intimacy, and generate new spatial relationships. As people pass through the space, their interactions layer over one another like stages in an ongoing evolution.

Undulating surfaces, concave and convex geometries, and vertical veils mutate the degrees of publicness within the space. Movement becomes dialogue, and the corridor becomes a living formation—an environment where bodies, perspectives, and ideas subtly reshape one another through motion.
