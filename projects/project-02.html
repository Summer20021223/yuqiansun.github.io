Conceptual – Architecture – “Vertical City”

2023

This project imagines a skyscraper as a vertical city along the Chicago River—an architectural world within a world. Instead of a single tower, the building operates as a multi-layered ecosystem where artists live, work, gather, and retreat. A monumental “core opening” forms the heart of the structure, functioning as a communal void that contrasts with surrounding zones of intimacy and privacy.

Developed through iterative massing studies, AI-generated floor patterns, facade investigations, and a physical section model, the design explores how complexity can be embedded within a seemingly simple exterior. It speculates on new forms of vertical urbanism where circulation, programs, and spatial narratives unfold in stacked layers of public, semi-public, and private worlds.
