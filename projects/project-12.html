Conceptual – “Hanging Memorial”

2023

This project is a memorial for the Italian writer Maria Valtorta, whose words return again and again to water—its power to purify, revive, nourish, and renew. In her poems, water becomes both physical and spiritual: it quenches the weary, restores what has withered, and stands as a metaphor for God’s grace. Because of this deep, lifelong connection, the memorial is nestled on Ponte alle Grazie and suspended gently above the Arno. The water beneath signifies revival and renewal, just as Valtorta wrote, while the river below becomes a quiet passage toward the heavens.

Floating between the living city and the moving current, the memorial transforms the bridge into a sacred pause—a place where memory rests lightly in the balance between the physical and the spiritual. The final delivery is a puzzle box containing information about the project, such as site and section diagrams embedded within, designed to be revealed upon exploration. The box can open, fold, and rearrange into the shape of a bridge—Ponte alle Grazie—with the massing model pinned onto it to convey the concept of a hanging memorial.
